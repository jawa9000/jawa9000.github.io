Retouch Work

Example of my digital retouch work at Memory Lane Portraits, Summer 2002

Historical recreation: 19th century downtown Savannah, Georgia

Several modern items were removed from the original photograph: Cars, parking meters, electric and reflective signs, electric lights, mailboxes, plants, select pipes, paneling, birds, people and cracks in the walls of the red building. Fourteen layers were used to restore, color correct and color balance the image to the nearst color scheme of the late 19th century.
