Modify Membership

Go to Deform > Edit Membership Tool to add/subtract vertices from the selected joint to create a better bend.

  • Select the joint you want to add vertices to.
  • Holding down the Shift key, select the vertices to add them to that joint.
  • Hold down Ctrl button while selecting vertices to subtract from the membership. Keep in mind; whenever you remove vertices from a membership of one joint, they have to be put back into another membership of another joint. Otherwise, you're end up orphaning vertices and when the root joint or any joints in that area moves, those orphaned vertices will stay behind.
  • By default, Rigid Binding does not flex the geometry. The vertices have an "all or nothing" relationship to the nearest joint. Because of this type of binding, the surface cannot easily deform with fluid motions
