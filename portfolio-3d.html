I used one of the animation files found in the super-awesome book Stop Staring: Facial Animation Done Right by Jason Osipa. Below, I have listed the playblasts from the resulting animations.

"Trippe, Man-eating Plant"

Character design process:

  • Modeling time = 2:18 hours
  • UV layout time = 1:44 hours
  • Texture time = 1:46 hours
  • Skeleton/IK creation = 35 minutes
  • Controllers creation/setup = 2:01 hours
  • Blend shape time = 1:16 hours
  • Enveloping time = 1:53 hours
  • Total Time = 11 hours and 33 minutes
  • Number of controllers = 24 controls with 91 keyable attributes (arms, fingers, head, body, legs, feet, speaking, eye emotions)
  • Number of Blend shapes = 17
  • 512 x 512 texture resolution
  • Polygon count = 1718 (proxy cage)
  • Model, textures, skeleton and lighting done in Maya 5.
